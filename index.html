Last updated: 2026-02-28

ShopGuard respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your information when you use the ShopGuard mobile application.

ShopGuard is designed for shopkeepers and small businesses to manage ledger, diary, loans, and reminders. ShopGuard is not a credit bureau, not a government service, and does not provide legal or financial advice.

1) Information We Collect

We may collect the following information:

A) Account Information

  • Email address (used for login and account management)
  • (Optional) Name (only if you choose to enter it)

B) User-Entered Business Data (Main App Data)

  • Customer/Supplier details (name, mobile number, notes, optional photo, optional profession)
  • Ledger entries (credit/debit), balances, payment history
  • Rozmel diary entries
  • Loan/Interest details
  • Cheque tracking details and reminders
  • Any notes or records entered by you

C) Attachments (Optional)

  • Photos, bills, or documents that you choose to upload inside the app

D) App Activity / Analytics Data

  • Basic app usage events (for performance and improvement)
  • We may calculate basic, non-personal statistics such as total active users (last 7/30 days), total customers count, and trial/expired user counts for monitoring and improving the app. These statistics are shown only as SAFE counts (no individual user list is displayed).

E) Device / Technical Data

  • Device model and Android version (basic diagnostics)
  • App version and basic diagnostic information (if applicable)
  • FCM token (for notifications)
  • Device identifiers (such as Firebase installation identifiers) used for analytics, diagnostics, and notifications

2) Customer Lists & Search Features (Owner List vs Public Search)

A) “My Customers” (Owner List)

  • This shows the customers that YOU have created/added in your own account.
  • Only you (the account owner) can see full details of your saved customers.

B) “Search Customer (10-digit)” (Public Search Mode)

  • This search works only when an exact 10-digit mobile number is entered.
  • This feature is intended only for identification and record matching.
  • In public search mode, ShopGuard shows only limited information required for basic identification. Sensitive details (such as full photo, full profession, or reporter identity) may be hidden or masked.

Important: ShopGuard does not verify whether user-entered customer information is true or accurate. Users are responsible for verifying any details before making business decisions.

3) How We Use Your Information

We use your data only to provide and improve app features, such as:

  • Rozmel diary
  • Customer/Supplier ledger
  • Loan/Interest tracking
  • Cheque reminders
  • Attachments
  • Backup/restore (if available)
  • App notifications and important updates
  • Technical support and bug fixing
  • Analytics for app performance and improvement

We do not sell or rent your personal data to anyone.

4) Subscriptions / Trial / Payments (Google Play Billing)

ShopGuard may offer a 90-day free trial for new users. After the trial ends, premium features may require an active subscription.

  • Subscriptions (if enabled) are purchased only by the user through Google Play Billing.
  • No automatic payment happens when the trial ends. Users must choose to buy a plan.
  • We do not store your full payment card/UPI details in our servers. Payment processing is handled by Google Play.

5) Does ShopGuard Share Data With Third Parties?

No selling. No advertising sharing.

ShopGuard does not sell user data and does not share user data with third parties for advertising or marketing. However, ShopGuard uses Google Firebase and related Google services as service providers to run app functionality. These services process data only to provide required features, such as:

  • Authentication (login/signup)
  • Cloud database sync
  • File storage (attachments)
  • Push notifications (FCM)
  • Analytics

6) Permissions and Device Features We Use

A) Microphone (Optional)

  • Used for voice entry in Rozmel / Cheque / Notes
  • Audio is not stored by ShopGuard

B) Camera / Photos (Optional)

  • Used only when you choose to attach bills/photos/documents or capture a photo

C) Notifications

  • Used for cheque reminders and important updates

D) Exact Alarm (Android 12+)

  • Used to trigger cheque reminder notifications at the correct time

E) Storage / Media Access (Optional)

  • Used only to let you pick images/documents for attachments

F) Contact Picker (No Contacts Permission)

  • ShopGuard may allow selecting a phone number using the system contact picker.
  • We do not request Contacts permission.

You can use basic app features even without optional permissions where possible.

7) Firebase / Third-Party Services

ShopGuard uses Google Firebase services, including:

  • Firebase Authentication
  • Cloud Firestore
  • Firebase Storage
  • Firebase Cloud Messaging (FCM)
  • Firebase Analytics
  • Firebase Cloud Functions

These services may process data as required for app functionality.

8) Data Security

  • Authentication
  • Firebase security rules
  • Encrypted data transfer (HTTPS/TLS)

However, no method of storage is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.

9) Data Retention

Your data is stored as long as:

  • Your account is active, and
  • You continue using the app

If you delete your account, your stored data will be deleted permanently (as described below).

10) Account Deletion and Data Deletion

Users can delete their account and associated data in two ways:

A) Inside the App (Recommended)
Settings → Delete Account & Data

B) By Contacting Support
If you cannot access the app, you may request deletion by email: shopguard.help@gmail.com

11) Children’s Privacy

ShopGuard is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

12) Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in this document and/or within the app where applicable.

13) Contact Us

If you have questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please contact us:

Email: shopguard.help@gmail.com

2) “My Customers” और “Search Customer (10-digit)” फीचर

ShopGuard में customer से जुड़े 2 view हैं:

A) “My Customers” (Owner List)

  • यहाँ आपके द्वारा जोड़े गए customers की list दिखती है।
  • आपके saved customers की पूरी जानकारी केवल आपको (owner) को दिखती है।

B) “Search Customer (10-digit)” (Public Search Mode)

  • यह search केवल exact 10-digit मोबाइल नंबर डालने पर ही काम करता है।
  • इसका उद्देश्य केवल पहचान (identification) और record matching है।
  • Public search mode में ShopGuard केवल basic पहचान के लिए limited जानकारी दिखा सकता है। Sensitive जानकारी (जैसे full photo, full profession, या reporter identity) छिपाई/मास्क की जा सकती है।

महत्वपूर्ण: ShopGuard user द्वारा डाली गई customer जानकारी की सत्यता verify नहीं करता। कोई भी business decision लेने से पहले जानकारी की जांच करना user की जिम्मेदारी है।
