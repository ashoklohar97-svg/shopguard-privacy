ShopGuard – Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-02-13   English + हिन्दी

ShopGuard respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your information when you use the ShopGuard mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following information:

  • Account Information: Email address (used for login and account management).
  • User-Entered Business Data: Customer/Supplier details (name, mobile number, photo, notes), ledger entries, Rozmel diary entries, amounts, transactions, loan/interest details, cheque reminders.
  • Attachments (Optional): Bills/photos/documents that you choose to attach.
  • Support / Technical Data (Basic): App version and device model may be used for bug reporting and support.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information only to provide app features such as Rozmel diary, customer/supplier ledger, loan/interest tracking, cheque reminders, attachments, backup/restore, app updates, and support. We do not sell or rent your personal data to anyone.

3. Permissions and Device Features We Use

ShopGuard may use the following device features only for app functionality:

  • Microphone (Optional): For voice entry in Rozmel / Cheque / Notes.
  • Camera / Photos (Optional): To attach bills/photos and documents.
  • Notifications: To show cheque reminders and important updates.
  • Exact Alarm (Android 12+): To trigger cheque reminder notifications at the correct time.
  • Contact Picker (No permission): ShopGuard may allow selecting a phone number using the system contact picker. We do not request contacts permission.

You can use basic app features even without optional permissions (where possible).

4. Third-Party Services

ShopGuard uses trusted third-party services such as Firebase by Google (Authentication, Firestore Database, Storage, Cloud Messaging). These services may process data as required to provide functionality.

5. Data Security

We use reasonable security measures such as authentication and Firebase security rules to protect user data. However, no method of storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

6. Data Retention

Your data is stored as long as your account is active and you continue using the app.

7. Account Deletion and Data Deletion

Users can delete their account and associated data in two ways:

  • Inside the App (Recommended): Settings → Delete Account & Data (permanent deletion of account and app data).
  • By Contacting Support: If you cannot access the app, you may request deletion via email below.

8. Children’s Privacy

ShopGuard is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in this document.

10. Contact Us

If you have questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please contact us:

Email: shopguard.help@gmail.com

1. हम कौन-सी जानकारी लेते हैं

हम निम्न जानकारी ले सकते हैं:

  • खाता (Account) जानकारी: ईमेल पता (Login और account management के लिए)।
  • उपयोगकर्ता द्वारा डाला गया Business डेटा: Customer/Supplier की जानकारी (नाम, मोबाइल नंबर, फोटो, नोट्स), ledger entries, Rozmel diary entries, amount और transactions, loan/interest की जानकारी, cheque reminder की जानकारी।

2. हम आपकी जानकारी का उपयोग कैसे करते हैं

हम आपकी जानकारी का उपयोग केवल app की सुविधाएँ देने के लिए करते हैं जैसे Rozmel diary, customer/supplier ledger (उधार खाते), loan/interest tracking, cheque reminders, attachments, backup/restore, app updates और support। हम आपका डेटा किसी को बेचते या किराये पर नहीं देते।

3. Permissions और Device Features का उपयोग

ShopGuard निम्न device features केवल app के काम के लिए उपयोग कर सकता है:

  • Microphone (Optional): Rozmel / Cheque / Notes में voice entry के लिए।
  • Camera / Photos (Optional): Bills/Photos/Documents attach करने के लिए।
  • Notifications: Cheque reminders और important updates दिखाने के लिए।
  • Exact Alarm (Android 12+): Cheque reminder को सही समय पर चलाने के लिए।
